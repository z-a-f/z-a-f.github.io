Meet ManagAI's AI Assistants

Discover the perfect AI companion to elevate your management effectiveness, from personal assistants to autonomous team guides.

Assist

An AI assistant that supports your day-to-day activities. From individual planning and scheduling to note-taking, Assist helps you stay organized and efficient.

Copilot

Copilot integrates into your management workflow, offering proactive suggestions and facilitating cross-team communication. It includes all Assist features plus team-level planning and insights.

Guide

Guide operates independently to manage individual meetings, cross-team collaborations, and resource allocations. It encompasses all Copilot functionalities with added autonomy.

Boost

Boost is our most capable AI assistant, ideal for managing large teams and complex projects. It offers all Guide features plus strategic decision-making capabilities like promotions, demotions, and dynamic priority adjustments.
